Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 Short version: TikPublish runs entirely on your own computer. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to any server we control. Your TikTok credentials stay on your machine.

1. Who We Are

TikPublish is a personal desktop application for individual content creators. It is developed and maintained by Aaryaman Kotwal. If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at aaryamankotwal@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

TikPublish does not collect any personal information. The application runs locally on your computer and has no connection to any TikPublish-operated server or database.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token (stored in a local .token file)
  • Your TikTok Client Key and Client Secret (stored in your local .env file)
  • Your TikTok display name, used only to show your connected account in the app interface

None of this information is sent to us or any third party other than TikTok's own API servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Because we do not collect your data, we do not use it. All API calls made by TikPublish go directly from your computer to TikTok's servers using your own credentials. TikPublish acts purely as a local interface.

4. TikTok's Data Practices

When you connect TikPublish to TikTok, TikTok may collect data about your usage in accordance with their own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with any third parties. There is no information to share — it never leaves your device.

6. Data Security

Your credentials are stored in plain text files on your local machine (.env and .token). You are responsible for keeping these files secure. We recommend not sharing your TikPublish folder with others and adding these files to .gitignore if you use version control.

7. Children's Privacy

TikPublish is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of TikPublish after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at aaryamankotwal@gmail.com.
